About Us

We are a team of researchers from Cardiff University: Gabriela Zapata-Lancaster, Tom Smith, and Nervo Verdezoto Dias.

We have been working together with Schools, children and teachers in Wales to better understand how we can make school buildings more energy efficient and sustainable.

We want children to help us collect data, and learn themselves about being more sustainable and making indoor environments (like your classroom) as good as they can be.
