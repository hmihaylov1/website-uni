Hot and Cold: Exploring Temperature

In this workshop, you'll become a temperature explorer and measure the heat in different places around our school. You'll use thermometers to measure temperatures in the classroom, the cafeteria, and even outside. You'll then analyze your data to discover where it's hottest and coolest in our school, and explore how temperature affects our daily activities.
