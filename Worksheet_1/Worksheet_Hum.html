The Humidity Experiment: What Makes the Air Wet?

Have you ever felt sticky or sweaty on a hot day? That's because of something called humidity, which is the amount of moisture in the air. In this workshop, you'll learn how to measure humidity and explore how it affects our bodies, our homes, and the world around us.
