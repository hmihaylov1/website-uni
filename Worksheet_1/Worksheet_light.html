Shine a Light: Exploring Brightness and Darkness

Light is all around us, but have you ever stopped to wonder how it works? In this workshop, you'll explore the properties of light, including brightness and darkness, and learn how to measure light using different tools. You'll also discover how light affects our lives, from the colors we see to the plants that grow around us.
